1. Summary of Refer-A-Friend Rewards Program

Connect with your friends and family and earn points to earn towards available rewards of your choice. When you refer friends get 10 points for every new friend that joins and makes a purchase. You can redeem rewards by mobile order, scanning receipt or scanning QR bar code at register. We elect to provide you with a unique referral link (“Referral Link”) that identifies you as the referral source for prospective Rewards Program customer (“Prospective Customer“). You are permitted to distribute your Referral Links to Prospective Customers for the purpose of promoting the Rewards Program. Distribution of your Referral Link may be made by the “Share” button on the referral page (“Invite Friends”) in the Conscious Kitchen's mobile application. If a Prospective Customer uses your Referral Link to register for a new Rewards Program account and makes a purchase under his/her Rewards Program account, you will receive a referral bonus, as determined by us. Without notice to you, Conscious Kitchen’s reserves the right to invalidate Referral Links at any time (including if we suspect fraudulent or improper use). Please make sure you have read “Detailed Rewards Program Terms Of Use.” Located to the drawer menu “Coupons and Rewards”
